About brumJS

This will be a meetup for programmers in and around Birmingham who use JavaScript heavily, lightly, for fun or profit. Anyone is welcome, regardless of experience.

This meetup is organised by Jack Wearden. Questions can be directed to him, or @brumJS on Twitter.
